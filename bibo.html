In this theme we learned about different styles and details we need to look at when creating a character. Physical, psychological and sociological traits and features are crucial to be analized in details.

Giving this character a story is important to look at the message you're going to present and state a clear genre/mood. The structure of the story must consist of setup, confrontation and resolution.

We were introduced to many narrative structures and forms and I followed the Aristotle narrative curve which consisted of prelude, point of no return, action, rising action, climax and fade out because it provided me a clear detailed storyline.

By being introduced to this whole process I understood the importance of many detailed steps which starts with an idea and sketching.
