XD AND WEB PROTOTYPE

Theme 04: Basic UX

04.02.01. Design sprint, 04.03.01. Web prototype

In this theme we had to create a new improved LMS system for Fronter after analyzing current problems during design sprint week.

Analyzing target groups their problems and wishes, testing their experience led us to look for solutions.

During this process we had to follow 5 days plan which consisted of mapping, sketching, deciding, prototyping and testing. This brought new experience working as a UX designer and taught us that the first solution that comes to mind needs way more analyzing and researching.

Working in a group where everyone has different ideas taught to look for compromise which sometimes requires picking ones that you may not agree with.
