Theme 03: Basic Animation

03.03.02. Interactive animation

In this theme we needed to apply our knowledge of CSS and JS to create a short animation based on a part of excisting story or create our own. Me and my partner decided to create the story ourselves and present the idea in the storyboard and also to draw our own charcters so we could improve our creative skills in these fields.

We learned to chain functions with events so the animation could continue without that much user interaction. We were introduced to sequence diagram which helped to structure and think about our program before writing it. Following the diagram helped us not to get lost in coding and keep a clear form.

Choosing to leave pseudo codes while making the foundation for code consumed time and made the work easier.
